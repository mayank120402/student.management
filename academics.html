Our Academic Programs

At Greenfield International School, we follow the CBSE curriculum with enhancements that provide our students with a competitive edge. Our academic programs are designed to develop critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills while maintaining a strong foundation in core subjects.

Pre-Primary (Nursery - KG)

Our play-based learning approach focuses on developing language, cognitive, motor, and social skills through interactive activities and exploration.

  • Theme-based learning
  • Phonics program
  • Montessori activities
  • Creative expression

Primary (Classes 1-5)

We build strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and scientific thinking while encouraging curiosity and independent learning.

  • Integrated project work
  • Reading enhancement program
  • Math lab activities
  • Environmental studies

Middle School (Classes 6-8)

Students transition to subject-specific learning with specialized teachers, developing deeper understanding and analytical skills.

  • STEM integration
  • Language enrichment
  • Social science projects
  • Skill-based electives

Secondary (Classes 9-12)

Rigorous academic preparation for board exams with career guidance and subject specialization options.

  • Science, Commerce & Humanities streams
  • Competitive exam preparation
  • Research projects
  • Career counseling

Our Teaching Methodology

At Greenfield, we employ a blend of traditional and innovative teaching methods to cater to diverse learning styles:

Interactive Learning

Classroom discussions, debates, and Q&A sessions to encourage participation and critical thinking.

Technology Integration

Smart classrooms, educational apps, and online resources to enhance learning experiences.

Collaborative Projects

Group assignments and team-based learning to develop cooperation and communication skills.

Experiential Learning

Laboratory work, field trips, and hands-on activities to reinforce theoretical concepts.

Differentiated Instruction

Customized teaching approaches to address individual learning needs and paces.

Critical Thinking Focus

Problem-solving exercises and case studies to develop analytical abilities.

Assessment Pattern

Our comprehensive assessment system evaluates both academic performance and holistic development:

Primary (Classes 1-5)

  • Continuous informal assessments
  • Portfolio-based evaluation
  • Skill-based grading
  • No formal exams till Class 3
  • Two term-end assessments from Class 4

Middle School (Classes 6-8)

  • Periodic tests (20% weightage)
  • Notebook submission (5%)
  • Subject enrichment (5%)
  • Term-end exams (70%)
  • Co-scholastic area assessments

Secondary (Classes 9-12)

  • Unit tests (20 marks each)
  • Pre-board exams for Classes 10 & 12
  • Practical assessments for Science/Commerce
  • Project work evaluation
  • CBSE board exam preparation
